==$\sigma$-代数==

集合在可数操作下还是封闭的性质

类比空间的结构性质.

set $X$ , $A\subseteq P(X) $ is a $\sigma$-algebra, if $X\in A$ and $A$ is closed under the operations of taking $()^c$ and $\bigcup/ \bigcap$.

  1. $X\in F$
  2. $A\in F \Rightarrow A^c\in F$
  3. $A_n\in F,n=1,2,…\Rightarrow \bigcup^{\infin}_{n=1}A_n\in F$

==可测映射==

类比开集的映射是开集等拓扑概念

定义：

$(X,m),(Y,n)$

A map $f: X\to Y$ is $(m,n)$-measurable.

if $\forall S\in n$ , $f^{-1}(S)\in m$ .

性质：

给一个子集族，就能找到包含这个的最小 $\sigma$-algebra

X: a set; $S\subseteq P(X)$: a family of subsets of X.

$A(S):=\bigcap{A|A\ \text{is a $\sigma$-algebra on X containing S}}$

is the smallest among all $\sigma$-algebra A on X containg S

典型例子

Borel代数:$B_x=A({C|C \ \text{is a closed subset of X}})$

Borel可测：$(B_X,B_Y)$ -measurable
