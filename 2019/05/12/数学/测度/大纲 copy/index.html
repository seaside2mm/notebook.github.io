==可测映射==

类比开集的映射是开集等拓扑概念

定义：

\((X,m),(Y,n)\)

A map \(f: X\to Y\) is \((m,n)\)-measurable.

if \(\forall S\in n\) , \(f^{-1}(S)\in m\) .

性质：

给一个子集族，就能找到包含这个的最小 \(\sigma\)-algebra

X: a set; \(S\subseteq P(X)\): a family of subsets of X.

\(A(S):=\bigcap\{A|A\ \text{is a $\sigma$-algebra on X containing S}\}\)

is the smallest among all \(\sigma\)-algebra A on X containg S

典型例子

Borel代数:\(B_x=A(\{C|C \ \text{is a closed subset of X\}})\)

\(Y\) 可测，X最小\(\sigma\)-algebra.

\(X\) 可测，Y最大\(\sigma\)-algebra.

A ==measure space== \((X,m,\mu)\) consists of a measurable space \((X,m)\) and a positive measure \(\mu\) on \((X,m)\).

If \(\mu(X)=1\), \((X,m,\mu)\) is a ==probability space==.
